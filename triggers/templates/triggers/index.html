Taking the first step toward understanding your addiction triggers is a brave and crucial move—and this app is here to support you on that journey. Recovery is not just about abstinence; it's about building self-awareness and developing practical strategies to navigate the complexities of daily life.

Triggers can be sneaky. They might be a sudden flash of stress, a familiar smell, or a pattern you don't even realize you're repeating. By logging your experiences and coping strategies, you’ll uncover patterns that help you respond with strength instead of impulse.

Start by noting the “who, what, when, and where” of each challenging moment and how you felt afterward. Over time, these reflections will become your personal roadmap—highlighting your challenges and your victories.
